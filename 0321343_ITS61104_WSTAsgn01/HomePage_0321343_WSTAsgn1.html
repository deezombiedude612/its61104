Welcome to the Plants vs. Zombies Plants Almanac!

Whether you are a Plants vs. Zombies fan, a regular Plants vs. Zombies player or not, this is where you can find out more about the plants used in the game!

This site is still under construction; there are still plants to be put in here, but as of now, this highlights some of the ones players would encounter first in the game.

Plants vs. Zombies is a tower defense game released by PopCap Games. The plants are the protagonists in the game, and the player's main defense against the undead assiliants. There are over a 100 playable plants in total, but only 33 are featured in this site, and counting. Some are available after finishing various levels in the game, while others are meant to be collected from Epic Quests, or to be purchased with gems (a rare currency in the game), or to be bought with real cash.

Various Statistics (as based from PvZ 2):
  1. Recharge Time:
    • Fast - 5 seconds
    • Mediocre - 10 or 15 seconds
    • Sluggish - 20 or 25 seconds
    • Slow - 30 or 35 seconds
    • Very Slow - 45\60\75 seconds
  2. Damage Rates:
    • Light - 0.25 to 1 normal shots (1/2x speed)
    • Normal - 0.25 to 1 normal shots
    • Moderate - 1.5 to 3 normal shots
    • Heavy - 4 to 6 normal shots
    • Huge - 27.5 to 50 normal shots
    • Massive - 60 or more normal shots
  3. Toughness Rates:
    • Typical - 2\3\6 bites from zombies
    • Elevated - 12\24\30\40\60 from zombies
    • High - 80 bites from zombies
    • Very High - 160 bites from zombies
